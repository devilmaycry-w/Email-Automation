Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Quick Summary: CodexCity helps you automate email responses using AI. We only access your Gmail to read incoming emails and send replies based on your templates. We never share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Name (from Google OAuth, if provided)
  • Profile picture (from Google OAuth, if provided)

Gmail Data

  • Incoming email content (subject, body, sender)
  • Email metadata (timestamps, message IDs)
  • OAuth tokens for Gmail access

Usage Data

  • Email classification results
  • Response templates you create
  • Automation logs and statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email Automation: Classify incoming emails and send automated responses
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve AI classification
  • Account Management: Maintain your account and provide customer support
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

3. Gmail API Usage

CodexCity's use of information received from Gmail APIs adheres to the Gmail API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specific Gmail Permissions:

  • Read emails: To classify incoming messages
  • Send emails: To send automated responses
  • Modify emails: To mark emails as read/processed

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption at rest
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Access to your data is limited to essential operations only

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users
  • With service providers who help operate our service (under strict confidentiality)

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Email logs: Retained for 90 days for service improvement
  • OAuth tokens: Automatically refreshed, old tokens are deleted
  • You can request data deletion at any time

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect Gmail access at any time

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication
  • Gmail API: For email access and sending
  • Supabase: For secure data storage
  • Netlify: For application hosting

9. Children's Privacy

CodexCity is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using CodexCity, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@codexcity.com
  • Through our application support system
Data Protection Officer: For EU users, you can contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@codexcity.com for any privacy-related inquiries.
