Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2025

This privacy policy outlines how our application ("CodexCity - AI Email Automation") uses and protects information when users connect their Gmail accounts to automate email tasks.

1. What Information We Collect

Upon user consent via Google Sign-In, we access the following Gmail metadata:

  • Email subject lines
  • Sender's email addresses
  • Timestamp of received emails
  • Message IDs for tracking
  • Email body content for AI classification purposes

We process email content solely for automated response generation and do not store full email bodies permanently.

2. How We Use This Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Trigger email automation workflows defined by the user
  • Classify emails using AI for appropriate response selection
  • Display email summaries and analytics in the user dashboard
  • Generate and send personalized automated responses via Gmail API
  • Provide usage statistics and performance metrics

3. Data Storage and Security

Email metadata and processing logs are securely stored in our backend using Supabase with industry-standard encryption. All data is associated only with the user who authorized access. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with row-level security
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited data retention policies

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your Gmail data or metadata with any third parties. All access is user-initiated and scoped to that individual user only. We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics for research purposes, but never personal or identifiable information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Users can request deletion of their stored email metadata at any time by:

  • Contacting us via email (see contact information below)
  • Using the account deletion feature in the application
  • Revoking access through Google Account permissions

Upon deletion request, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Revoking Access

You may revoke our access to your Gmail account at any time via Google Account Permissions. This will immediately stop all automated processing of your emails.

7. Educational and Commercial Use

This application is designed for legitimate business email automation purposes. Users must comply with applicable laws and regulations, including CAN-SPAM Act and GDPR requirements.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or through the application interface.
